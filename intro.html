About Me

Hello! I'm Shah Jalal Dip, an enthusiastic Computer Science undergraduate at American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB), currently in my 6th semester. I’m passionate about building software that solves real-world problems and enhances user experience. Over the past year, I've immersed myself in competitive programming, sharpening my problem-solving skills through platforms like Codeforces and LeetCode. I enjoy tackling challenging algorithmic problems and constantly strive to improve my logical thinking and coding abilities. In parallel, I’ve been developing my skills in web development, working on full-stack projects using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and the PERN stack. One of my recent projects includes a Weather App built with PERN, showcasing both frontend design and backend integration. I’ve also worked on academic projects such as a University Dashboard and an E-Commerce Management System, applying object-oriented principles and database design without relying on external frameworks. Whether it’s through coding, debugging, or deploying applications, I enjoy every step of the development journey. I’m eager to keep learning, contributing to open-source, and eventually grow into a skilled software engineer.
